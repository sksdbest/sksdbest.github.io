What is MIST Lab?

We are a group of researchers from IIT Bhilai, lead by Dr. Sk. Subidh Ali, dedicated to secure AI as well as IoT products.The focus of our group is to develop solution to secure machine learning enabled product against AML attacks and to make world and internet a secure place

Learn More

Machine Learning

Machine learning (ML) is a branch of AI and computer science that focuses on the using data and algorithms to enable AI

Adversial ML

Adversarial machine learning is the study of the attacks on machine learning algorithms, and of the defenses against such attacks

Cyber Security

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting internet-connected systems such as hardware, software and data from cyberthreats.
